About The Event

Dear Teacher,

Sometimes, even when you're having a good time, you can't help but stop and think about how much you miss the old times. School life wouldn't be the same, if you weren't a part of that journey.

You were not only our teacher, but also our friend, our guide and a true wellwisher all rolled up into one person. We find ourselves so fortunate enough to call you as our teacher because only teachers feel genuinely happy when we fly sky-high.

Here, we are back to our old little homes, saying thanks to these wonderful teachers

Let's celebrate this joy and more success to everyone.

Things to Remember

Here is a list important things you need to keep in your mind.

Registrations

Every student who wish to attend the reunion Must be registered in advance. Hence, please feel free to get your name registered if it's not done yet.

Timings

We request all the students to be present at the venue by 4:00 PM to start the event on time.

Seating

We have reserved few front row seats for your beloved teachers, so please feel free to ask volunteers if you have any questions

Volunteers

We have plenty of volunteers who can help you with your queries, please reach out to them and get your questions answered anytime during or before the event

Rules at School

Let us ensure that the school premises is kept clean through out our visit. During your visit to class rooms, let us not distrub the order of things kept at the rooms.

Rules at Venue

1. Please provide your name and batch to the volunteers when you are asked of this information. This is done for identification and crowd controlling purposes only considering it is a paid event.
2. Since there is lack of sufficient parking at event site, please avoid four wheelers.
